My Story




Hello there! My name is Katelyn Nguyen.

I'm a second-year student at the University of Waterloo in Global Business and Digital Arts + I love anything soup/broth related.

My favourite dishes are the ones that have broth or liquid. Although I enjoy trying and tasting all kinds of foods, my heart always falls for dishes with warm, rich broths.

During my first year of university I lived on residence and away from my family and friends. Being further away from my loved ones made me homesick quite often. Moreover, when it came to cooking, my amateur skills could not remotely compare to my mother’s.

These factors impacted my food consumption and I struggled with maintaining my meal plan money. Luckily, my residence had a kitchen and I used it to my advantage.

The First Dish



The first dish I ever made was the Korean dish soondubu jiggae also known as tofu stew. I would say it was more of an imitation though, since I was missing many key ingredients.

Regardless, it marked my start with cooking, and from that point on, I began creating all sorts of other simple dishes. Currently, my go-to dish is just a simple instant chicken broth, egg, whatever vegetables I have in the fridge, and rice.

I’m not a professional chef or anything but I'm consistently improving each day with my cooking. It may sound silly but a warm bowl of soup always helps wash away my worries or struggles. Even though it's temporary, I find those moments comforting.

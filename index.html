My Story




Hello there! My name is Katelyn Nguyen.

I’m currently in my second year student in GBDA at the University of Waterloo + I love anything soup-related.

My favourite dishes are the ones that have broth or liquid. Although I enjoy trying and tasting all kinds of foods, my heart always falls for dishes with warm, rich broths.

During my first year of university I lived on residence and away from my family and friends. Being further away from my loved ones made me homesick quite often. Moreover, when it came to cooking, my amateur skills could not remotely compare to my mother’s.

These factors impacted my food consumption and I struggled with maintaining my meal plan money. Luckily, my residence had a kitchen and I used it to my advantage.
